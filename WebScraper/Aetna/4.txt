Aetna Inc. the nation's leading health insurer, named Dr. William C. Popik as its chief medical officer as it struggles to control costs and change the way it deals with customers and doctors. Dr. Popik, 55, will start March 5 and report to Aetna's chief executive, John Rowe, the company, which is based in Hartford, said. Dr. Popik had been senior vice president and national medical director at Cigna HealthCare, a unit of the Cigna Corporation, the health insurer. Dr. Popik will be at the center of Aetna's efforts to manage medical costs.
