Banco Hispano Americano said it would pay 9.7 billion pesetas, or about $100 million, to the Aetna Life and Casualty Company for its 45 percent stake in the insurance company La Estrella. La Estrella's chairman, Miguel Geijo, said the priceamounted to 45 percent of the company's capital, reserves and potential capital gains plus a 23 percent premium. Aetna estimated it would realize a capital gain of about $32 million for its interest in La Estrella. The transaction is expected to be completed in May.
