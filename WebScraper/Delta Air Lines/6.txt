Delta Air Lines has begun a review for an agency or agencies to handle creative and media duties on its account. Creative duties are handled by BrightHouse Live, Atlanta, part of BrightHouse, while media duties are handled by Starcom Worldwide, Chicago, part of the Publicis Groupe.
Andy McDill, a spokesman at Delta in Atlanta, confirmed a report of the review on Adweek.com. BrightHouse Live and Starcom will participate in the review, Mr. McDill said, which is being managed by the Rojek Consulting Group in Cleveland.
