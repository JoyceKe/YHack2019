Berkshire Hathaway Inc. said today that its profit rose 32.7 percent in 1984, to $148.9 million, or $129.82 a share, from $112.2 million, of $105.15 a share, in 1983. Revenue rose to $729.2 million in 1984 from $602.7 million in 1983, it said. Last week, Berkshire Hathaway's chairman, Warren E. Buffett, agreed to pay about $517 million for 18 percent of the stock in the company that would result from the proposed merger of the American Broadcasting Companies into Capital Cities Communications Inc.
