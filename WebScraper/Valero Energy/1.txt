The Valero Energy Corporation of San Antonio announced that its Valero Natural Gas Company subsidiary had filed a registration statement with the Securities and Exchange Commission for an offering of $100 million principal amount of sinking fund debentures due in 2003.
Valero said its subsidiary would use the proceeds to repay advances from the parent company and to finance a capital expansion program. The parent company's share is to be applied to repayments of outstanding bank credit lines, Valero said. Underwriters for the issue are led by Lehman Brothers Kuhn Loeb Inc.; Goldman, Sachs, and Rotan Mosle Inc.
