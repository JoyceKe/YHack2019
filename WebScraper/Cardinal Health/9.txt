The medical products and services company Cardinal Health said on Monday that it would buy the medical technology company Viasys Healthcare for $1.42 billion, an acquisition that would help Cardinal expand in international markets.
Cardinal will pay $42.75 a share for the outstanding shares of Viasys, a 35 percent premium over its closing price of $31.55 on Friday. Shares of Viasys rose nearly 37 percent, or $11.63, to $43.18 Monday. Cardinal will also assume $50 million in debt.
