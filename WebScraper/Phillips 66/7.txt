Mr. Phillips was president and chairman of the National Council for Industrial Defense, a nonprofit group concerned with military issues. He was staff director of the House Foreign Operations and Government Information Subcommittee from 1971 to 1975 and staff director of the Committee on House Administration from 1977 to 1981.
His survivors include his wife, Margaret Noll Phillips; two daughters, Susan L. Phillips of Silver Spring, Md., and Patricia P. Crowl of Tuscaloosa, Ala.; two stepsons, Mark McDermott and Sean McDermott of Potomac; and two grandchildren.
