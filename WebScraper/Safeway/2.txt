Safeway Stores Inc. said it was planning to raise its stake in the Vons Companies to 35 percent from 30 percent. Last month, Safeway acquired 6.7 million Vons shares as part of a $410 million transaction in which Vons acquired Safeway's operations in Southern California. Vons is a supermarket chain based in El Monte, Calif. Safeway, based in Oakland, Calif., made the disclosure in a Securities and Exchange Commission filing. It has agreed not to acquire more than 35 percent of Vons's shares.
