Safeway Stores Inc., based in Oakland, Calif., said that it had agreed to settle all claims asserted in various shareholder suits regarding the supermarket chain's pending acquisition by a new company formed by Kohlberg, Kravis, Roberts & Company and Safeway top management. Under the agreement, Safeway's board declared a cash dividend of 25.5 cents a share, payable Sept. 30 to holders of record at the close of business on Aug. 28, and a second dividend of 17 cents a share, payable Sept. 30 to holders of record as of Sept. 2.
