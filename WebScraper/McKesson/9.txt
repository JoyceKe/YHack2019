Mr. Harlan joined McKesson in 1974 and has been chairman for 10 years; he also served as president and chief executive. Mr. Field joined the company in 1984 as president and chief operating officer and added the title of chief executive in July 1986, succeeding Mr. Harlan.
McKesson also said that it had agreed to sell certain assets of its Carlton Importing Company, the exclusive United States importer and marketer of St. Pauli Girl beer, to the Hiram Walker Company. The transaction is subject to regulatory approval.
