Plains All American Pipeline L.P., a subsidiary of the Houston-based Plains Resources Inc., plans to buy a West Texas crude oil pipeline and gathering system from the Chevron Pipe Line Company. for about $40 million. Under terms of the deal, Plains All American would gain around 400 miles of crude oil pipelines and storage capacity for three million barrels of crude oil in Texas. Plains All American recently agreed also to buy Scurlock Permian L.L.C. and certain other pipeline assets from Marathon Ashland Petroleum L.L.C. Scurlock's key asset is an 800-mile pipeline in West Texas.
