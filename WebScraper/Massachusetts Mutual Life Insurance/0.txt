Assets of the Massachusetts Mutual Life Insurance Company rose $44,379,888 last year to $810,336,717. The gain was largely invested in government bonds, which exceeded $95,000,000 at the year-end. Net earnings rose $2,183,942 to $15,759,213 and policies increased in value by $29,701,327, so that insurance in force at the end of 1942 aggregated $2,055,144,876 for 532,915 policies, not counting more than 40,000 annuity policies.
