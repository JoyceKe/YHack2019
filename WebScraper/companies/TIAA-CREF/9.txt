TIAA-CREF, the financial services company, said yesterday that it had narrowed a review for its account to three finalists. The account, with billings estimated at more than $20 million, has been handled for more than 15 years by Ogilvy & Mather Worldwide in New York, part of the WPP Group .
Stephanie Cohen Glass, a spokeswoman at TIAA-CREF in New York, confirmed a report in Adweek.com that identified the finalists as Ogilvy, Modernista in Boston and TBWA/Chiat/Day in New York, part of the TBWA Worldwide unit of the Omnicom Group . One agency, Siltanen/Keehn in El Segundo, Calif., was eliminated from the review, which is expected to be completed next month.
They are the Chicago office of Campbell Mithun, part of the McCann-Erickson World Group division of the Interpublic Group of Companies ; Euro RSCG Tatham Partners in Chicago, part of the Euro RSCG Worldwide division of Havas ; Grant Jacoby in Chicago; the Chicago office of Hoffman York; J. Walter Thompson Chicago, part of the WPP Group; and the Chicago office of Young & Rubicam Advertising, part of the Young & Rubicam division of WPP.
