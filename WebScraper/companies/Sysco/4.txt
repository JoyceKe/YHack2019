The Sysco Corporation said it had agreed to acquire the Beaver Street Foods business of Beaver Street Fisheries Inc., a privately held importer of frozen seafood and meat based in Jacksonville, Fla. Terms were not disclosed. Beaver Street Foods, a food-service distribution operation, has annual sales of $130 million a year, Sysco said. Sysco, a food-service marketing and distribution company based in Houston, had sales of $11.3 billion for the nine months that ended on March 28. Sysco shares rose 56.25 cents yesterday, to $24.1875.
