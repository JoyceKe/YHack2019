The buyout unit of J.P. Morgan Chase & Company's agreed to acquire Safety-Kleen Europe, a British maker of solvents and cleaners for the automotive industry, for about £275 million ($510 million). Electra Partners Europe, a buyout firm, is selling Safety-Kleen, which employs 1,200, J.P. Morgan said. Safety-Kleen had sales of about £100 million in the 12 months ended June 30. Electra Investment Trust will receive about £100 million from the sale of its stake.
