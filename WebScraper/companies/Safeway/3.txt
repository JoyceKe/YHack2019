Safeway Stores Inc. said that it was eliminating or consolidating about 300 administrative and clerical positions, affecting about 25 percent of the employees at the company's headquarters in Oakland, Calif., and its supply operations complex in Walnut Creek, Calif. Safeway, the world's largest supermarket chain, employs about 172,000 workers. ''Because of recent events, our company is facing a major restructuring of operations,'' Peter A. Magowan, Safeway's chairman, said in a statement.
