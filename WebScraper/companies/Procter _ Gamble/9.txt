Nathan Benjamin Cardin and Benjamin Butin Procter were married Saturday evening at the Annenberg Community Beach House in Santa Monica, Calif. William Scott Calvert, a friend of the couple who was appointed a deputy marriage commissioner by the state, officiated.
Mr. Cardin (left), 31, is a chemistry teacher in the upper school of Harvard-Westlake School, an independent day school in Los Angeles. He graduated magna cum laude from Dartmouth and received a Ph.D. in organic chemistry from Stanford.
He is a son of Christine M. Bowler and Peter J. Bowler of Gilford, N.H. His mother is an associate at Bank of New Hampshire in Laconia. His father retired as an Air Force master sergeant at Hurlburt Field, Fort Walton Beach, Fla. He now teaches mathematics at Rundlett Middle School in Concord, N.H.
He is a son of Sandra B. Procter and David E. Procter of Manhattan, Kan. His parents work at Kansas State, where his mother is an assistant professor in the Department of Human Nutrition, and his father is the director of the Center for Engagement and Community Development.
