Concentra, based in Addison, Tex., has annual revenue of $800 million. It provides occupational medicine, urgent care, physical therapy and wellness services at more than 300 medical centers in 42 states.
“We are excited about the opportunity to acquire a strong stand-alone business that reinforces our core businesses while providing both revenue diversification and opportunities for strategic expansion longer term,” Michael B. McCallister, Humana’s chairman and chief executive, said in a statement.
