The Columbia-HCA Healthcare Corporation of Louisville, Ky., the nation's largest hospital chain, said yesterday that it would acquire certain assets of Goodlark Regional Medical Center Inc. for $103 million. Columbia plans to acquire the 176-bed Goodlark Regional Medical Center and Town and Country Home Health Care in Dickson, Tenn., as well as the 29-bed Cheatham Medical Center in Ashland City, Tenn. Columbia-HCA's stock dropped 75 cents yesterday, to $42.75, on the New York Stock Exchange.
