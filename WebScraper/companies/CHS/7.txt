CHS Electronics Inc. agreed yesterday to buy Karma International S.A., a Swiss distributor of personal computer components, for $160 million in cash and stock. CHS, based in Miami, said it would pay $74 million in cash and $86 million worth of stock. CHS shares gained 75 cents yesterday, to $26.50. CHS, a microcomputer products distributor, said the acquisition was subject to the completion of CHS's recent offering of up to 10 million common shares. Part of the proceeds from the offering would finance the acquisition. Last year, Karma earned $15.1 million on revenue of $700 million.
