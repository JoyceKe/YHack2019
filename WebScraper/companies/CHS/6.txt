CHS Electronics Inc. plans to buy four European personal computer subsidiaries of Metro A.G. of Germany for about $320 million. CHS, a computer-products distributor based in Miami, said the purchase price consisted of $295 million in cash and $25 million in stock. It will also assume an undisclosed amount of debt. CHS Electronics said the units that it was acquiring had combined 1997 sales of $2.3 billion. CHS Electronics also disclosed that it had purchased most of the shares of Metrologie International of France, a European distributor of hardware and software, on June 30. CHS Electronics had 1997 sales of $4.76 billion. Metro, which is based in Cologne, Germany, has annual sales of about $60 billion.
