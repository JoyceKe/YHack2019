The computer distributor CHS Electronics Inc. said yesterday that it had agreed to acquire three computer-equipment distribution businesses in Latin America for cash and stock. The Miami-based CHS said the purchase price would be based on the acquired businesses' future performance. The companies to be acquired have estimated combined 1998 sales of $330 million. The largest of them is Intcomex, which has annual sales of more than $200 million and operations in Chile, Guatemala, Mexico, Panama, Peru, Uruguay and Miami. CHS will also acquire Acron S.A. of Argentina, with sales of $32 million, and Cornejo Informatica S.A. of Argentina, with sales of $21 million. CHS distributes computers, peripherals and software.
