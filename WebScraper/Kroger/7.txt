Net income for the quarter was $66.9 million, or 77 cents a share, compared with a loss of $45.9 million in the comparable period of 1989. Revenues were $4.99 billion, up 7 percent from $4.65 billion in the 1989 quarter.
For the year, Kroger reported net earnings of $82.4 million, or 95 cents a share, compared with a loss of $72.7 million in 1989. Revenues for 1990 were $20.26 billion, up 6 percent from $19.1 billion in 1989.
