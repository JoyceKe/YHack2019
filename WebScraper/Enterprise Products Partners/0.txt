The debt ratings of Enterprise Products Partners were cut to the lowest investment grade by Standard & Poor's on concern about the partnership's weaker financial performance. Ratings on about $1.8 billion in debt at the Houston-based pipeline partnership were lowered one level to BBB- from BBB and may be cut again, S.&P. said in a statement yesterday The credit rating company said the outlook was negative for Enterprise Products Partners. The partnership said it planned to sell $100 million of Class B nonvoting limited units to an affiliate of the Enterprise Products Company in December, with the proceeds to be used to reduce debt.
