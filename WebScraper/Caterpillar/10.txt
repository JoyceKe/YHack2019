Caterpillar Industrial Inc., a unit of Caterpillar Inc., and Mitsubishi Heavy Industries are considering a venture to make lift trucks and other material-handling equipment. The companies have signed a memorandum of understanding to explore the formation of the company, Caterpillar said. Products would be sold in the United States and abroad under the Caterpillar and Mitsubishi brand names. Mitsubishi Heavy Industries would probably own 80 percent of the proposed venture, the company said.
