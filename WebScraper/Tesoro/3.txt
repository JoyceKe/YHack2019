The Tesoro Petroleum Corporation of San Antonio has been ordered to pay more than $56 million to the Energy Department for regulatory violations between 1973 and 1981, the company said today. According to the agency's final order, Tesoro has 30 days to make an initial payment of $25 million, after which six annual payments of $5.2 million will be due. The Energy Department had sought a total of $144 million in fines and repayment for the energy company's reported abuse of price and allocation regulations during the eight-year period.
