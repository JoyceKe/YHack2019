Liberty Financial confirmed yesterday, without further comment, that it was holding discussions to acquire Wanger Asset Management, owner of the Acorn group of five mutual funds. The Wall Street Journal reported yesterday that Liberty had agreed to acquire Wanger for about $450 million, or about 6 percent of Wanger's $7.3 billion of assets under management. Liberty Financial, based in Boston, has $67 billion in assets and owns 95 mutual funds, including the Stein Roe and Newport Pacific Management groups. It is majority owned by the Liberty Mutual Insurance Company. Liberty Financial's stock fell $1.25 a share, to $22.50.
