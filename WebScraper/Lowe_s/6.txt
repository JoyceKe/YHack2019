Lintas once was an acronym that stood for Lever International Advertising Services, after the marketer Unilever ( news/quote ); Lintas was its in-house agency until the agency was acquired by the Interpublic Group of Companies ( news/quote ).
In 1994, Interpublic merged Lintas with Ammirati & Puris, forming Ammirati Puris Lintas. The company then merged that agency with Lowe & Partners in 1999 to form Lowe Lintas. Lowe will continue to be a unit of the Partnership division of Interpublic.
• Rino Scanzoni was named president for broadcast for North America at Media Edge, New York, part of the WPP Group ( news/quote ), effective on Jan. 2. He succeeds Robert Igiel, who retired. Mr. Scanzoni had most recently been executive vice president and managing director at the New York office of MediaVest Entertainment, a division of the United States operations of MediaVest, which is part of the Starcom MediaVest Group unit of the Bcom3 Group.
