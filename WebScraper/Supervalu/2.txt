In a deal that could far-reaching implications for the supermarket industry, Supervalu announced Thursday, that Ron Burkle’s private equity fund Yucaipa is putting $680 million into the Minneapolis-based supermarket chain, the second-largest in the country.
It marks Mr. Burkle’s third major investment in a supermarket chain in a little more than a year, and many investors are now wondering if he will roll up several companies, a move he has done in the past.
