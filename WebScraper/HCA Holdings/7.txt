Berkshire Hathaway Inc., the investment company run by Warren E. Buffett, reduced its stockholdings in the First Data Corporation, the Duke Energy Corporation and Iron Mountain Inc. during the second quarter, according to regulatory filings. Berkshire also reported that it owned 16.6 million shares of HCA Inc., the hospital chain, as of June 30, a stake worth about $532 million, yesterday's filings showed. Berkshire, based in Omaha, reported the holdings after the Securities and Exchange Commission denied the company's request to keep them confidential, Berkshire said in the filing.
