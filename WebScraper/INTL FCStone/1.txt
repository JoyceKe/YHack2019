Lauren Elizabeth Sandberg Springer, a daughter of Dr. Barbara R. Sandberg and Steven M. Springer of New York, is to be married Sunday to Daniel Charles Rosen, a son of Dr. Sharon R. Maza and Eric D. Rosen of Teaneck, N.J. Rabbi Jeremy Wieder is to officiate at Old Westbury Hebrew Congregation in Old Westbury, N.Y.
The bride, 26, is an associate with the New York law firm Weil, Gotshal & Manges, specializing in technology and intellectual property. She graduated summa cum laude from the University of Pennsylvania.
Her father, who is retired, worked in New York as the chief financial officer of the commodities subsidiary of Intl FCStone, a financial services firm. Her mother is an educational psychologist in New York.
His mother is a cardiologist at Cardio Care Associates in Hackensack, N.J. His father is an information technology director for the News Corporation in New York, maintaining circulation and advertising systems.
